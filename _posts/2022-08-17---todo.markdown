<p>点击开通：</p>

<p><img height="326" src="/uploads/ckeditor/pictures/236/image-20220817154226-1.png" width="1384" /></p>

<p>选择使用方式</p>

<p><img height="409" src="/uploads/ckeditor/pictures/237/image-20220817154253-2.png" width="755" /></p>

<p>可以选择跳过然后进行了解：</p>

<p><img height="955" src="/uploads/ckeditor/pictures/238/image-20220817154349-3.png" width="1811" /></p>

<p>点击申请签名即可进行信息的填写：</p>

<p>选择验证码或者通用（适用验证码、短信通知、推广短信、国际/港澳台短信）</p>

<p><img height="906" src="/uploads/ckeditor/pictures/239/image-20220817154557-4.png" width="985" /></p>

<p>如果选择通用和自用，就不需要太多的信息：</p>

<p><img height="682" src="/uploads/ckeditor/pictures/242/image-20220817154809-7.png" width="717" /></p>

<p>提交，就会进行审核</p>

<p><img height="752" src="/uploads/ckeditor/pictures/243/image-20220817155105-8.png" width="1076" /></p>

<p>点击添加模板，就可以进行模板的添加了</p>

<p><img height="864" src="/uploads/ckeditor/pictures/244/image-20220817155159-9.png" width="1551" /></p>

<p>如果不知道怎么进行模板的书写，就看一看官方示例</p>

<p><img height="828" src="/uploads/ckeditor/pictures/245/image-20220817155510-10.png" width="1132" /></p>

<p>填写完毕，右侧会出现一个发送的示例，可以看是否是需要的</p>

<p><img height="955" src="/uploads/ckeditor/pictures/246/image-20220817155640-11.png" width="1722" /></p>

<p>提交就会进入审核。</p>

<p><img height="846" src="/uploads/ckeditor/pictures/247/image-20220817155724-12.png" width="1093" /></p>

<p>然后就自动跳转</p>

<p><img height="381" src="/uploads/ckeditor/pictures/248/image-20220817155750-13.png" width="1586" /></p>
